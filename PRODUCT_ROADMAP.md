# POPUP Product Roadmap

## North Star

Build a mobile-first onchain discovery app where digital products are browsed with the familiarity of Instagram and collected with the simplicity of one tap plus wallet confirmation.

## Phase 1: Foundation

Goal:
Establish the product structure and remove friction-heavy or mock-driven behavior.

Deliverables:

- desktop landing page
- mobile-first app shell
- bottom navigation with Home, Discovery, Marketplace, Profile
- wallet connect only on action
- remove hardcoded fake content from core UX
- reliable rendering for PDF, image, and downloadable tool cards

Success markers:

- app loads cleanly on desktop and mobile
- no dead navigation
- no fake production states in key flows

## Phase 2: Discovery Core

Goal:
Make POPUP feel alive as a content and creator discovery product.

Deliverables:

- story-style Home experience
- Instagram-like Discovery feed
- creator card identity system
- likes
- gift/share action
- creator profile entry from cards
- product preview modal or screen

Success markers:

- improved browse depth
- more content interactions before wallet connect

## Phase 3: Collect and Access

Goal:
Make digital product collection intuitive and trustworthy.

Deliverables:

- collect flow with just-in-time wallet connect
- free collect and paid collect paths
- gated access after collect
- PDF reader in app
- image full view
- downloadable tool access flow

Success markers:

- higher collect conversion from viewed products
- low drop-off on wallet connect step

## Phase 4: Creator Tools

Goal:
Make creators able to publish and manage without needing a separate admin-heavy experience.

Deliverables:

- creator dashboard
- upload and publish flow
- product management
- earnings view
- product analytics
- creator token launch flow

Success markers:

- repeat creator publishing
- creator token launches completed end-to-end

## Phase 5: Marketplace

Goal:
Enable peer-to-peer resale for creator tokens without cluttering core discovery.

Deliverables:

- creator token marketplace
- token detail screen
- list for sale flow
- buy flow
- transaction history

Success markers:

- active secondary listings
- repeated marketplace participation

## Phase 6: Social and Retention

Goal:
Add lightweight retention loops without overwhelming the product.

Deliverables:

- saved products
- better creator profile depth
- notification center
- personalized discovery ranking

Success markers:

- stronger 7-day retention
- more returning collectors

## Build Priorities

1. Mobile discovery UX
2. Preview and collect flow
3. Creator dashboard basics
4. Marketplace separation and clarity
5. Social retention loops
